# Edulia User Guide

## Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Go to https://edulia.angelstreet.io
2. Click on a demo account card, or enter your email and password
3. You'll be taken to your role-specific dashboard

### Navigation
- **Left sidebar** shows all available modules for your role
- **Top bar** shows your name and organization
- **Notifications** bell icon shows unread messages

## For School Administrators

### Viewing School Stats
Your dashboard shows live counts of teachers, students, parents, and classes. Attendance for today is shown below.

### Managing the Timetable
1. Go to **Emploi du temps** in the sidebar
2. View the weekly schedule grid (Mon-Sat, 8h-18h)
3. Sessions are color-coded by subject
4. The system detects scheduling conflicts (teacher, room, or group double-booking)

### Sending Announcements
1. Go to **Messages**
2. Click **Nouveau message**
3. Select recipients (entire class, specific parents, or teachers)
4. Write your message and send

## For Teachers

### Entering Grades
1. Go to **Notes** in the sidebar
2. Select a class from the dropdown
3. Click **Nouvelle évaluation** to create an assessment
4. Enter scores for each student
5. Students and parents see grades immediately

### Downloading Report Cards
Students and parents can download PDF report cards from the Grades page. The PDF includes:
- Per-subject grade breakdown
- Weighted averages
- General average
- Auto-generated appreciation (Très bien, Bien, Assez bien, etc.)

### Assigning Homework
1. Go to **Devoirs**
2. Click **Nouveau devoir**
3. Select class, subject, and due date
4. Add description and optional attachments

## For Students

### Checking Your Grades
1. Go to **Mes notes**
2. See all subjects with their averages
3. Click on a subject to expand and see each individual assessment
4. Download your report card as PDF using the **Bulletin PDF** button

### Viewing Your Schedule
Go to **Emploi du temps** to see your personal weekly timetable.

## For Parents

### Viewing Your Child's Grades
1. Go to **Notes**
2. If you have multiple children, select the child from the dropdown
3. View grades per subject with expandable assessment details

### Communicating with Teachers
1. Go to **Messages**
2. View existing conversations or start a new thread
3. Messages show sender names and timestamps

## EduliaHub

### Browsing Courses
1. Go to https://eduliahub.angelstreet.io
2. Browse the catalog of 31 courses across 15 platforms
3. Filter by category, platform, difficulty level, or language
4. Click a course for full details

### Tracking Your Learning
1. Create an account on EduliaHub
2. Subscribe to courses you're interested in
3. Rate courses you've completed (1-5 stars)
4. Build your certification portfolio

### Public Portfolio
Your certificates are visible at your public portfolio URL. Share it with employers or colleagues.
